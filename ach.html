This patent introduces a portable ECG and SpO2 monitoring device that enables real-time cardiac health monitoring. Best Paper Presentation Award at SSITCON-2024
Ms. Neena K A and Dr. Anil Kumar M N, faculty members of the ECE department, were awarded the "Best Paper Presenter" award for their paper titled "A Lightweight Deep Learning Framework for Brain Tumour Classification from Compressed MRI Images" at the International Conference on Software, Systems, and Information Technology (SSITCON-2024). The conference was held on 18th–19th October 2024, at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, Tumakuru.
Noble G
First Prize at VLSID Design Contest
Mr. Noble G., Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, FISAT, secured the first prize at the prestigious VLSID Design Contest—India’s premier conference in Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) design. Competing against top researchers and industry professionals, his innovative work and technical brilliance earned him the top honor. This exceptional achievement brings great pride to the ECE department and highlights FISAT’s growing presence in advanced technology and research.
