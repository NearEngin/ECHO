Patents From Electronics and Communication Department

Jeslin P Jo
Real-Time Health Monitoring System: Portable ECG & SpO2 with Cloud-Based Doctor interaction
This patent introduces a portable ECG and SpO2 monitoring device that enables real-time cardiac health monitoring. It features ECG waveform acquisition and blood oxygen level mesurements, allowing users to monitor their health at home or on the go, with cloud-based doctor interaction for continuous healthcare support.
Jilu George
Loud Cell Based Smart Security and Anti-Theft Alert Alarm Sytem and Application
This patent features an innovative anti-theft flooring system that integrates a load cell with an ESP32 microcontroller to detect unauthorized access attempts, offering enhanced security and alert functionalities

Anil Kumar
Best Paper Presentation Award at SSITCON-2024
Ms. Neenu K A and Dr. Anil Kumar M N, faculty members of the ECE department, were awarded the "Best Paper Presenter" award for their paper titled "A Lightweight Deep Learning Framework for Brain Tumour Classification from Compressed MRI Images" at the International Conference on Software, Systems, and Information Technology (SSITCON-2024). The conference was held on 18th–19th October 2024, at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, Tumakuru.
Noble G
First Prize at VLSID Design Contest
Mr. Noble G., Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, FISAT, secured the first prize at the prestigious VLSID Design Contest—India’s premier conference in Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) design. Competing against top researchers and industry professionals, his innovative work and technical brilliance earned him the top honor. This exceptional achievement brings great pride to the ECE department and highlights FISAT’s growing presence in advanced technology and research.
Jilu George
ECE Faculty Recognized for NPTEL Mentorship
Ms. Jilu George, Assistant Professor in the ECE Department, received a Certificate of Appreciation for her outstanding mentorship in the NPTEL Online Course "Introduction to Programming in C" during the January- April 2024 session. The department congratulates her for her dedication in guiding students through essential programming concepts.
